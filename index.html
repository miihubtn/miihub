Services

  • Public Transport Demand & Supply Design

  • Urban Redevelopment & Accessibility Impact Analysis
  • Traffic Calming & Sustainable Urban Mobility Planning
  • Multimodal Hub Design & Pedestrian Flow Simulation

Featured Projects

  • Belluno Public Transport Simulation: Designed multiple lines and optimized depot and terminal locations for rural-urban areas.
  • Milano 30: Modeled 30 km/h speed zones to analyze effects on safety, emissions, and modal shift.
  • Multimodal Hub – Milano Bovisa: Pedestrian simulation using VISSIM to improve station flow, LOS, and infrastructure redesign.
